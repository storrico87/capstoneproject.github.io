Bringing Students and Community Together!

Hand-n-Hand Volunteering is a non-commercial volunteering website created by two graduating students (Spencer Torrico and Joey Hawilah) at ASU Polytechnic. The goal of this website is to help students, both new and continuing, find volunteering opportunities in the local area for the purpose of helping their community, getting the change to meet other students and members of the community, and obtaining critical volunteer time to satisfy scholarship requirements.

By Students, For Students

While this site is for non-commercial use and not ASU affiliated, we are proud to say that all the work that went into this project was done using the knowledge obtained during our time at ASU. Our hope is this will exemplify the coursework ASU can provide for future students!
